Full Feature List

Tasks

Notifications
Get notified of tasks at a certain time once or on a set interval.

Searching
You can search through your tasks using the search bar at the top of the task list.

Automatic Cleaning
Finished tasks are automatically cleaned. If you choose, you can disable this in the settings.

Notes

Markdown
You can use Markdown Formatting in your notes. Most markdown syntax is supported.

Notebooks
Organize your notes by putting them into separate notebooks.

Tags
Add custom tags to your notes, allowing you to further organize them.

Colors
Even further organize your notes by coloring their titles.

Searching
Search across notebooks by name or by tag.

Calendar

Widget
Notorious provides its own calendar widget, allowing you to see items from the home screen.

Item Names
You can change the calendar dots to the actual names of the calendar items in the settings.

Other

Backups
You can back up all of your data from Notorious, making transfers to another device easy.
